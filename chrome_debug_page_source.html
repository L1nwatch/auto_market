2nd CHANCE - FEBRUARY 2025 PROMOTION RULES

  1. DEFINITIONS

    1. 2nd CHANCE PROMOTION(hereinafter the “Promotion”):

      Promotion held pursuant to the Sales promotion games By-law (CQLR,c. S-13.1, r. 4).

    2. LOTO-QUÉBEC:

      The Société des loteries du Québec, the party responsible for organizing the Promotion.

  2. ELIGIBILITY

    Only an individual who is eighteen (18) years of age or older and a resident of Québec may enter the Promotion provided they are the lawful holder of a valid and eligible draw-based lottery ticket or scratch ticket purchased in Québec (hereinafter the “Ticket”).

    All eligible lottery tickets are listed herein in Appendix A.

  3. DURATION

    The Promotion will run from midnight on February 1, 2025, until 11:59 p.m. on February 28, 2025.

  4. HOW TO ENTER

    1. Qualified entrants who have purchased a Ticket from a retailer may enter the current Promotion free of charge as long as they enter by 11:59 p.m. on February 28, 2025:

      • By using the Loto-Québec app and following the instructions to this effect,
        or
      • By going to the Promotion page (lotoquebec.com/2chance) and following the instructions to this effect.

      Qualified entrants who have purchased a Ticket online will automatically be entered in the Promotion.

    2. Any entry not submitted by 11:59 p.m. on February 28, 2025, will be considered void and will not be eligible for the current Promotion. The same applies to any entry that is erroneous, incomplete, fraudulent or otherwise in violation of these rules.

    3. Only Québec residents may enter the Promotion.

    4. Every dollar spent on the Ticket entitles the ticketholder to one (1) entry in the Promotion draw.

    5. A free draw-based lottery ticket is not eligible for the Promotion.

    6. Only one (1) entry per Ticket is permitted for the duration of the current Promotion and for any 2nd Chance Promotion.

  5. DRAW TERMS AND CONDITIONS, AND PRIZE DESCRIPTION

    1. The Promotion draw will be conducted electronically on March 3, 2025, from all the entries duly received, in accordance with these rules, using the Loto-Québec computer system at the Loto-Québec draw room in Montréal. Five (5) Promotion winners will be selected.

    2. The draw will be conducted by the Loto-Québec draw officer under the supervision of an external auditor.

    3. Individuals whose entries are selected will be contacted by phone by a Loto-Québec customer service agent, who will explain the terms and conditions for claiming their prize.

    SHOULD THE SELECTED ENTRIES BE VALID AND COMPLY WITH THESE RULES:

    1. In the event that the selected entries are valid and comply with these rules, the winners will each receive a prize of ten thousand dollars ($10,000), provided that they abide by the terms and conditions for claiming their prize within seventy-two (72) hours of being contacted by phone and that the information they provide matches that used when they entered the Promotion.

    SHOULD THE SELECTED ENTRY NOT BE VALID OR NOT COMPLY WITH THESE RULES:

    1. Should a selected entry not be valid and not comply with these rules, or should the person whose entry is selected not comply with the terms and conditions for claiming their prize within the prescribed deadline, in accordance with Section 5.4 hereof, such entry will be void and a new entry will be selected at random until a valid entry that complies with these rules is selected, in which case the terms and conditions in Section 5.4 will apply.

  6. PRIZE CLAIM TERMS AND CONDITIONS

    1. Loto-Québec requires that winners fill out a prize claim form and present one of the following ID documents:

      • Health insurance card
      • Driver’s licence
      • Passport

    2. The prize will be paid out to the winners in the form of a cheque.

  7. LAST NAME, FIRST NAME, REGION AND PHOTOGRAPH

    The winners’ first name, last name, region, photograph and any other information provided by them may be used by Loto‐Québec and its subsidiaries for the draw described herein and for advertising purposes, particularly on the Internet (including on social media). Winners may claim no rights to broadcasting, printed material, advertising or compensation of any kind in this regard.

  8. OTHER RESTRICTIONS AND CONDITIONS

    1. The prize must be accepted as is. It cannot be transferred or exchanged. Loto-Québec reserves the right to substitute the prize for another one of equivalent value.

    2. Loto-Québec reserves the right to cancel the Promotion, in whole or in part, or to delay it for any reason whatsoever and without prior notice, including in cases of fortuitous events or force majeure. Loto-Québec may also amend these rules at any time, in which case the amended rules will be made available to the public.

    3. Loto-Québec declines all liability should the Promotion be cancelled, postponed or amended, or in case of a computer system failure or malfunction.

    4. Loto-Québec declines all liability toward anyone who did not receive one or more electronic entries or a chance to enter the Promotion.

    5. Loto-Québec cannot be held responsible for the loss or theft of a Ticket or any other situation, incident or event that prevents anyone from meeting entry deadlines or claiming their prize.

    6. Should an entrant fail to comply with these rules, make a false statement, attempt to cheat, commit fraud, act in collusion with a third party or conspire with such third party, their entry will automatically be void, and they will be disqualified from further participation in any event related to the Promotion.

  9. DISPUTES

    Any dispute related to Promotion management and prize fulfillment will be settled according to these rules and the Sales promotion games By-law (CQLR, c S-13.1, r. 4).

  10. RULE AVAILABILITY

    The Promotion is subject to the detailed rules set forth herein, which are available at lotoquebec.com/2chance.
